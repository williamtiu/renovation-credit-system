About DecoFinance

🏠 Platform Introduction

DecoFinance is a renovation-finance platform built for lenders, suppliers, and customers who need a clearer way to verify contractors, score risk, and release funding against real project progress.

🎯 Core Modules

  • Decorator Trust Registry: Maintain verified company profiles, licences, references, insurance, and operational credentials.
  • Renovation Credit Scoring: Evaluate firms across financial strength, operating stability, credit behaviour, team readiness, and project risk.
  • Project-Backed Financing: Connect verified projects, bids, milestones, escrow logic, and underwriting decisions.
  • Risk Management Dashboard: Monitor score drift, exposure, disputes, overdue balances, and portfolio watchlists.
  • Audit And Compliance: Preserve consent, verification history, and reviewer actions for controlled lending workflows.

📊 Scoring Model

The DecoFinance score uses five dimensions tuned for the renovation sector:

Dimension Weight Max Points
Financial Strength 30% 300 Points
Operational Stability 25% 250 Points
Credit History 25% 250 Points
Team Assessment 10% 100 Points
Industry Risk 10% 100 Points

🔒 Security And Privacy

The platform records consent, reviewer decisions, and operational checkpoints so financing teams can work with stronger controls and traceability.

📞 Market Focus

DecoFinance is designed around Hong Kong renovation market pain points: fragmented contractor quality, uneven cashflow, milestone disputes, and limited lender visibility into live project risk.
