About RenoCredit

🏠 System Introduction

RenoCredit is a Renovation Credit System based on comprehensive credit scoring, helping renovation companies manage risk and loan applications.

🎯 Core Features

  • Company Info Management:Manage renovation company details.
  • Credit Scoring System:Evaluate based on 5 dimensions.
  • Loan Application Processing: Streamline the entire loan application lifecycle.
  • Risk Assessment: Identify and mitigate high-risk elements.
  • Data Analytics: Comprehensive dashboard for data visualization.

📊 Scoring Model

The credit score is based on 5 dimensions:

Dimension Weight Max Points
Financial Strength 30% 300 Points
Operational Stability 25% 250 Points
Credit History 25% 250 Points
Team Assessment 10% 100 Points
Industry Risk 10% 100 Points
